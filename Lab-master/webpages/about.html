About-Us

Our aim is to develop an digital health care report for an desktop based system. Scanned documents, pictures of lab reports which are stored in computers, and pictures of reports taken by any camera or scanner are the mainly focus of this application.The purpose of this application is to recognize text in scanned documents,text images,and any picture taken by camera in order to store scanned data in database for further use.It will beneficial to you to store information at particular location and we can access it if, it is needed instead of wasting time on retyping it.
